Services We Offer
Mole Removal

If you have a mole on your face or body that impacts your self-esteem, causes discomfort, or that you were advised to remove for medical reasons, mole removal may be a good option for you. During this fast procedure, we numb the area and gently remove the mole in its entirety so it doesn't grow back. There will be stitches in the operated area for approximately 1 week.

Cyst Removal

While sebaceous and epidermoid cysts are relatively harmless, they are often a cause of cosmetic concern. Our team uses skill and expertise to delicately remove cysts in their entirety so they don't grow back, all the while aiming to reduce scarring.

READ FAQs
Scar Revisions

Has a previous accident or surgery left you with obvious scarring? Our scar revision procedure will improve the appearance of your existing scar. During the procedure, we remove the old scar along with any remaining scar tissue. Then, the area is stitched back together in a thin, fine line. You can expect a fast recovery time and minimal to no scarring.
